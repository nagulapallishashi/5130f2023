WEB SECURITY AND SOLUTIONSAbstract: The rapid evolution of the internet has transformed the way we live, work, and communicate. In this digital age, web applications have become an integral part of our daily lives, facilitating everything from online shopping to social networking, and from banking to healthcare. However, the convenience and connectivity offered by the web have also given rise to a myriad of security challenges. Ensuring the security of web applications is now a fundamental imperative, as the threats they face are as diverse and dynamic as the online worlditself. This paper is dedicated to exploring different solutions to improve web security.
